Proactively identify hidden chronic conditions in asymptomatic patients—$0 start‑up cost.

Pulse4Pulse empowers physicians to proactively identify disease indicators in asymptomatic patients by utilizing cutting‑edge preventive testing and technologies while improving the overall patient experience.

  • Instant, non‑invasive testing in under 20 minutes.
  • Five billable diagnostic codes, integrated billing support.
  • Over 2,000 physicians. In business over nine years. Located in Middlebury, CT.

How It Works

  1. Patient comes into practice for their regular appointment.
  2. Patient completes intake form (eligibility screen).
  3. Pulse4Pulse medical technician conducts combined ANS, ABI, and Sudomotor tests.
  4. Results are immediately available for the provider’s review.
  5. Patient follow‑up visit scheduled.
  6. Practice receives claims/remittances & P4P invoices per test.

Key Tests & Benefits

ANS Testing

Measures heart rate variability and autonomic adaptability. Uses BP cuffs, EKG, and SpO₂ sensors; patient performs breathing exercises while monitored.

Benefit: Identify autonomic dysfunction before symptoms appear.

ABI Testing

Detect early peripheral vascular disease. Uses BP cuffs on arms and legs, EKG leads, and SpO₂ sensors for two minutes on each side.

Benefit: Spot peripheral artery disease in asymptomatic patients.

Sudomotor Testing

Activates sweat glands to detect microvascular neuropathy.

Benefit: Find neuropathy early, before clinical signs.
