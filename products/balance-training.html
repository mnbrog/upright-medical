Improve Strength, Stability, and Confidence

Our Balance Training Program targets core stability, proprioception, and functional lower‑limb strength through a series of evidence‑backed exercises.

  • Guided by licensed physical therapists.
  • Customizable protocols based on patient risk level.
  • Progress tracking with quantitative metrics.

Sample Exercises

  • Seated Marching: Improves hip flexor activation and core engagement.
  • Tandem Stance: Challenges static balance by standing heel‑to‑toe.
  • Heel‑to‑Toe Walk: Develops gait stability.
  • Single Leg Reach: Enhances proprioception on one limb.
