Seamless Telehealth for Preventive Screenings

  • HIPAA‑compliant video visits for remote FRA and balance assessments.
  • Real‑time data capture of self‑reported metrics.
  • Integrated scheduling with major telehealth platforms.
  • Secure cloud storage of results and reports.

Technical Requirements / Setup Guide

Minimum tech requirements:
– Desktop or mobile device with webcam and microphone
– Stable internet connection (≥ 5 Mbps upload/download)
– Latest Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge browser

To get started:

  1. Sign up for a provider account.
  2. Download the Telehealth App from our portal.
  3. Configure device settings (camera, mic).
  4. Complete tutorial session with our onboarding specialist.
  5. Start scheduling remote visits.
